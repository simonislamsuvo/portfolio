InverPrime Project

"InverPrime: Advanced Pure Sine Wave Inverter Technology" is a project focused on designing and implementing a high-efficiency pure sine wave inverter that converts 12V DC into stable 220V AC. Developed under the supervision of Dr. Md. Kamal Hosain, the system integrates a DC step-up chopper for voltage regulation and a microcontroller-based control mechanism for precise operation and safety. Unlike modified sine wave inverters, "InverPrime" produces a smooth sine wave output, ensuring compatibility with sensitive electronic devices while maintaining high efficiency. The inverter features advanced protection mechanisms against overvoltage, undervoltage, and overload conditions, enhancing reliability and safety. By optimizing power conversion efficiency and implementing intelligent control algorithms, the project demonstrates a practical and robust solution for uninterrupted power supply applications.
